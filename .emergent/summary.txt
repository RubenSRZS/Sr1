The user, representing the renovation company Sr-Renovation.fr, requires a web application to create professional, highly customized quotes and invoices. The primary goal is to achieve a unique and brand-aligned design that distinguishes them from competitors, using their specific blue and orange brand colors.

**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:**
1.  **Core Functionality:** Create, view, and manage quotes and invoices.
2.  **Unique Design:** A professional and unique document design reflecting the brand identity.
3.  **Client & Catalog Management:** Manage clients and a catalog of services.

7.  **Live PDF Preview:** Users must see a live preview of the PDF document as they fill out the form.
8.  **Document Conversion:** One-click conversion from an accepted quote to a new invoice, with an option to mark it as paid.
9.  **Item Numbering:** Service items in the PDF should be numbered, with an option to disable it.
10. **State Persistence:** Form data should be saved automatically so work is not lost when navigating to other pages.
11. **Global Dark Mode:** A dark mode theme that applies consistently across all pages of the application.
12. **Catalog Enhancements:** Use color codes to categorize services and set default, editable units (m², ml, etc.) for each service.
